# Findings

The forensic examination produced the following findings:

- A BitLocker-encrypted data volume was successfully identified.
- Encryption status, encryption method, and protection settings were documented.
- BitLocker recovery-key information was successfully recovered and documented.
- The encrypted volume was accessible through authorized recovery procedures.
- A logical forensic acquisition of the encrypted volume was successfully completed.
- MD5 and SHA-1 hash values were generated for acquired evidence.
- Hash verification confirmed evidence integrity following acquisition.
- FTK Imager review confirmed successful preservation of collected forensic artifacts.
- Windows system version information was documented to support investigative context.

The collected evidence demonstrated successful acquisition and preservation of encrypted data while maintaining forensic integrity throughout the investigation.
