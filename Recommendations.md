# Recommendations

Based on the examination results, the following recommendations are provided:

- Maintain secure storage and protection of BitLocker recovery keys.
- Implement documented procedures for encrypted-media acquisition during investigations.
- Perform cryptographic hash verification before and after evidence acquisition.
- Preserve original encrypted media whenever possible to maintain evidentiary integrity.
- Maintain detailed acquisition logs documenting encryption status, recovery information, and collection procedures.
- Validate forensic images immediately following acquisition to identify integrity issues.
- Ensure investigators are trained in BitLocker recovery and encrypted-drive acquisition methodologies.
- Use forensic tools that support encrypted volume acquisition and validation within controlled forensic environments.

These practices help ensure reliable evidence preservation and support repeatable forensic examination procedures involving encrypted storage devices.
